Call Dick Strong

Dick Strong gets it. He knows his name is funny. It started in sixth and seventh grade. A private school classmate brought in a "Reader's Digest" article, featuring a mother who called her son Richard, "Dickie Bird." That's when the kids realized Dick was the go-to nickname for Richard. "And I had no choice in the matter," the 39-year-old said, grinning. "And so my choice was I can either fight against this, or I can go with it."
